How it stays honest
The app loads a pre-bundled snapshot of real NEON Surface Water Chemistry, so there are no live-API waits, and leads with transparent, well-sourced methods. Every statistic reports its sample size. Relationships use ordinary least squares with R², p and n, flagged for temporal autocorrelation; correlations default to Spearman (robust to skewed chemistry) with an n ≥ 8 reliability guard and a multiple-comparisons caveat. Seasonality is a real STL decomposition of the measured record, not a fitted sine wave. Below-detection values are flagged rather than hidden, and analyte names and units are chemically verified (bromide vs bicarbonate, chloride vs chlorine, ANC in meq/L).
